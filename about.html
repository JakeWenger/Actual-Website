A Little About Myself

I am a graduate from the University at Buffalo, where I pursued and obtained a degree majoring in geographic info science and a minor in environmental design. Originally going to school for civil engineering, I decided to pursue my degree in geographic info science due to the numerous fields that utilize GIS to analyze everyday problems and provide solutions to those problems. I felt that as I grow as a person with time, I’d better be able to learn and flourish in my field more so than I could with civil engineering or another discipline. It’s the versatility in regards to applying GIS to another field that ultimately pulled me towards this career path.

Currently, I’ve begun searching for my first job within the discipline of geospatial science, having moved and settled into the Phoenix area fairly recently. Although filled with a sense of nervousness, to a greater extent I’m filled with excitement at the thought of truly starting down my post-graduate path.

Further Info
